Overview

The POCO M7 Plus 5G stands out as a unique contender in the budget smartphone segment, primarily defined by its extraordinary 7,000mAh battery capacity. Designed for users who prioritize longevity and screen real estate, it features a sprawling 6.9-inch display that turns the device into a portable theatre. While it occupies a larger footprint than typical mid-rangers, it compensates with efficient thermal management and a stable, power-sipping Snapdragon 6s Gen 3 processor that handles daily tasks and casual gaming with ease.

Key Features

Beyond its endurance, the M7 Plus integrates premium software features usually reserved for flagship tiers, including Google's Circle to Search, AI Eraser, and AI Sky Editing. The 144Hz refresh rate provides an exceptionally smooth scrolling experience, and the inclusion of an 18W reverse charging capability allows it to double as a power bank for your other devices. With four years of security updates and two years of major Android OS upgrades, it is built to remain relevant long after purchase.
