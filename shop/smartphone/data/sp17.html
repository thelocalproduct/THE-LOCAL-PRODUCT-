Overview

The OnePlus Nord CE4 Lite 5G continues the legacy of the "Core Edition" series by focusing on essential performance and user experience. It sports a bright, vibrant AMOLED display that ensures excellent visibility even under direct sunlight, paired with high-frequency PWM dimming for eye comfort. While it sticks to the tried-and-tested Snapdragon 695 chipset, the optimization within OxygenOS makes the device feel fluid and responsive for social media, streaming, and light multitasking.

Key Features

A major highlight of this model is its endurance and fast charging capabilities. The massive 5500mAh battery combined with 80W SUPERVOOC charging means you spend significantly less time tethered to a wall outlet. The 50MP primary camera featuring OIS (Optical Image Stabilization) is a standout in this segment, capturing crisp images even with shaky hands. Furthermore, the inclusion of "Aqua Touch" technology allows for better screen responsiveness even when the display or your fingers are wet.
