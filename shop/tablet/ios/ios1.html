Design & Performance

Redesigned from the ground up, the 5.1mm chassis is Apple's thinnest product ever. The M4 chip provides desktop-class performance, handling 4K video editing, multitasking, and AAA gaming (Resident Evil Village, Death Stranding) with ease thanks to hardware-accelerated ray tracing and mesh shading.

Display Experience

The Tandem OLED display is the device's standout achievement. With a 2,000,000:1 contrast ratio and deep inky blacks, it provides color accuracy unmatched in the tablet space, making it a dream for creative professionals.

Pencil Pro & Productivity

The new Apple Pencil Pro introduces squeeze gestures, haptic feedback, and barrel roll functionality. While the new Magic Keyboard adds a larger glass trackpad and function row, the software (iPadOS) remains the bottleneck for those hoping to replace a full macOS desktop setup.
